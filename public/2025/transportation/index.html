Transportation Guide

2 min

Getting to the Venue#

Huazhi Hotel (No. 97 Kexue Avenue, Zhongyuan District, Zhengzhou) is reachable by taxi or metro from the city’s main transport hubs. Use the routes below for the smoothest arrival experience.

From Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport#

Taxi

  • Distance: ~55 km
  • Duration: ~1 hour
  • Boarding point: Ride-hailing pickup area at Terminal 2

Metro

  1. Enter at Xinzheng Airport Station (Exit B).
  2. Take the Suburban Line to Nansihuan Station.
  3. Transfer to Line 2 toward Zijingshan Station.
  4. Transfer to Line 1 toward Zhengzhou University Station and exit at B.
  5. Walk 100 m west to the hotel entrance.
  6. Total duration: ~2 hours.

From Zhengzhou East Railway Station#

Taxi

  • Distance: ~28 km
  • Duration: ~40 minutes
  • Boarding point: Taxi/ride-hailing pickup area outside the station concourse

Metro

  1. Enter the metro hall inside Zhengzhou East Station.
  2. Take Line 8 directly to Zhengzhou University Station and exit at E.
  3. Walk 30 m straight to the venue lobby.
  4. Total duration: ~46 minutes.

From Zhengzhou Railway Station#

Taxi

  • Distance: ~16 km
  • Duration: ~30 minutes
  • Boarding point: West Square ground-level parking/taxi area

Metro

  1. Enter at Zhengzhou Railway Station (Exit B).
  2. Take Line 1 toward Zhengzhou University Station and exit at E.
  3. Walk 30 m straight to the venue lobby.
  4. Total duration: ~40 minutes.

For late-night arrivals, taxis or ride-hailing services remain available around the clock. Please allow extra time during peak commuter hours.
