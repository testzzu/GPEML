Conference Proceedings#

The proceedings of GPEML will be published and made available to all registered attendees.

Publication Information#

  • Publisher: To be announced
  • Format: Digital and Print (if applicable)
  • ISBN: To be assigned
  • DOI: To be assigned

Access#

All registered attendees will receive access to the digital proceedings. Proceedings will be available:

  • Online through the conference website
  • Via download link sent to registered attendees
  • On USB drive (if provided at the conference)

Paper Format#

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings following the Springer LNCS format guidelines.

Indexing#

The proceedings will be submitted for indexing in:

  • DBLP
  • Google Scholar
  • ACM Digital Library (if applicable)
  • IEEE Xplore (if applicable)

Copyright#

Authors of accepted papers will be required to sign a copyright transfer agreement. Details will be provided upon paper acceptance.

Open Access#

Authors may have the option to make their papers open access. Additional fees may apply. More information will be provided upon acceptance.
