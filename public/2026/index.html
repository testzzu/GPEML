GPEML 2026

Next edition · details to be announced

Building on Zhengzhou#

Beginning in 2025, the Genetic Programming and Evolutionary Machine Learning Symposium becomes an annual gathering. The 2026 edition will extend the Zhengzhou momentum with a broader geographic reach, hybrid participation options, and new partners across academia and industry.

What to Expect#

  • Expanded thematic tracks on evolutionary deep learning, interpretable ML, and automated program synthesis
  • Joint sessions with allied computational intelligence societies to mirror the collaborative spirit of flagship Pacific Rim events
  • Fresh showcases of industrial deployments plus tutorials aimed at early-career researchers

Timeline (Tentative)#

  • Q1 2026: Call for papers, tutorials, and workshops released
  • Q2 2026: Program committee and keynote lineup announced
  • Q3 2026: Final venue logistics, registration details, and proceedings information published

Exact dates and host city will be confirmed soon. Institutions interested in co-organizing or sponsoring should contact the steering committee to receive the hosting brief and planning milestones.
