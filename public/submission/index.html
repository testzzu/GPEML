Paper Submission#

All paper submissions must be made through the conference submission system.

Submission System#

Submit your paper here (Link to be updated)

Submission Guidelines#

Format Requirements#

  • Format: Springer LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science) format
  • Page Limit: Maximum 12 pages (including references)
  • File Format: PDF only
  • Language: English

Paper Categories#

  1. Regular Papers - Full research papers (12 pages)
  2. Short Papers - Work-in-progress or position papers (6 pages)

Review Process#

  • All papers will undergo a double-blind review process
  • Each paper will be reviewed by at least three program committee members
  • Reviews will be based on originality, technical quality, significance, and clarity

Important Dates#

  • Paper Submission Deadline: June 13, 2025
  • Extended Paper Submission Deadline: June 27, 2025 (Anywhere on Earth)
  • Acceptance Notification: August 22, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Submission: September 5, 2025

Submission Checklist#

Before submitting, please ensure:

  • Paper follows the LNCS format guidelines
  • Paper is within the page limit
  • All author information is removed for blind review
  • All figures and tables are clear and readable
  • References are complete and properly formatted
  • Paper has not been submitted elsewhere

Camera-Ready Submission#

Accepted papers must be submitted in camera-ready format by the deadline. Authors will receive detailed instructions upon acceptance.

Publication#

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings. Selected papers may be invited for publication in special issues of leading journals.

Contact#

For submission-related questions, please contact: submission@gpeml2025.org
