Call for Papers#

The GPEML Program Committee invites original, substantial, and unpublished technical papers on all areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Knowledge Representation and Reasoning
  • Planning and Scheduling
  • Multi-agent Systems
  • AI Applications

Submission Guidelines#

  • Papers should be submitted in PDF format
  • Maximum page limit: 12 pages (including references)
  • Format: Springer LNCS format
  • All submissions must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference

Important Dates#

  • Paper Submission Deadline: June 13, 2025
  • Extended Paper Submission Deadline: June 27, 2025 (Anywhere on Earth)
  • Acceptance Notification: August 22, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Submission: September 5, 2025

Call for Workshops#

We invite proposals for workshops to be held in conjunction with GPEML. Workshops provide a forum for discussion of specialized topics and offer an opportunity for focused interaction among participants.

Workshop Proposal Submission#

Deadline: April 22, 2025

Workshop proposals should include:

  • Title and description
  • Organizers’ information
  • Expected number of participants
  • Format and schedule

Call for Tutorials#

We welcome proposals for tutorials on topics of interest to the AI and ML community. Tutorials should provide comprehensive coverage of a specific topic.

Tutorial Proposal Submission#

Deadline: June 23, 2025

Tutorial proposals should include:

  • Title and abstract
  • Instructor(s) information
  • Target audience
  • Duration and format

Call for Sponsorship#

We welcome sponsorship from organizations interested in supporting GPEML. Sponsorship opportunities include:

  • Platinum Sponsor - Premium visibility and benefits
  • Gold Sponsor - High visibility and benefits
  • Silver Sponsor - Standard visibility and benefits
  • Bronze Sponsor - Basic visibility

For more information about sponsorship opportunities, please contact: sponsorship@gpeml2025.org

Call for Student Scholarship Program#

The “Students are the Future” scholarship program provides financial support for students to attend GPEML. Applications are welcome from graduate and undergraduate students.

Application Requirements#

  • Student status verification
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • CV/Resume

For application details, please visit the Student Scholarship Program page.
