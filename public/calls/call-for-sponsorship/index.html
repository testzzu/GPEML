Call for Sponsorship#

We welcome sponsorship from organizations interested in supporting GPEML. Your sponsorship helps make the conference accessible to researchers worldwide and supports the advancement of AI and ML research.

Why Sponsor GPEML?#

  • Visibility - Reach a global audience of AI/ML researchers and practitioners
  • Networking - Connect with leading researchers and industry professionals
  • Recruitment - Attract top talent in AI and ML
  • Brand Recognition - Enhance your organization’s profile in the AI community
  • Support Research - Contribute to the advancement of AI and ML research

Sponsorship Levels#

Platinum Sponsor - $XX,XXX#

Benefits:

  • Premium logo placement on conference website and all materials
  • Large banner display at conference venue
  • Complimentary conference registrations (10)
  • Exhibition booth space
  • Recognition in opening and closing ceremonies
  • Advertisement in conference proceedings
  • Logo on conference bags
  • Speaking opportunity (5 minutes)

Gold Sponsor - $X,XXX#

Benefits:

  • Prominent logo placement on conference website
  • Banner display at conference venue
  • Complimentary conference registrations (5)
  • Exhibition booth space
  • Recognition in opening ceremony
  • Logo on conference materials
  • Advertisement in conference program

Silver Sponsor - $X,XXX#

Benefits:

  • Logo placement on conference website
  • Banner display at conference venue
  • Complimentary conference registrations (3)
  • Recognition in conference materials
  • Logo on conference program

Bronze Sponsor - $XXX#

Benefits:

  • Logo placement on conference website
  • Complimentary conference registration (1)
  • Recognition in conference program

Custom Sponsorship Opportunities#

We also offer custom sponsorship packages for specific events:

  • Welcome Reception Sponsor
  • Conference Dinner Sponsor
  • Coffee Break Sponsor
  • Student Scholarship Sponsor
  • Best Paper Award Sponsor
  • Keynote Speaker Sponsor

Exhibition Opportunities#

Sponsors at Gold level and above can set up exhibition booths at the conference venue to showcase their products and services.

Benefits for All Sponsors#

  • Access to attendee list (with consent)
  • Opportunity to distribute promotional materials
  • Networking opportunities with conference attendees
  • Recognition in conference communications

How to Sponsor#

To become a sponsor, please contact us:

Email: sponsorship@gpeml2025.org

We will provide you with:

  • Detailed sponsorship package information
  • Custom sponsorship proposals
  • Payment and invoicing details

Sponsorship Agreement#

Upon agreement, sponsors will receive:

  • Sponsorship contract
  • Logo submission guidelines
  • Marketing material specifications
  • Timeline and deadlines
