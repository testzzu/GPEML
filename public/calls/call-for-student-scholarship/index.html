Student Scholarship Program - “Students are the Future”#

The GPEML Student Scholarship Program, “Students are the Future,” provides financial support to help students attend the conference. This program aims to make the conference accessible to students from diverse backgrounds and support the next generation of AI and ML researchers.

Program Benefits#

Scholarship recipients will receive:

  • Conference Registration Fee Waiver - Full waiver of conference registration fees
  • Accommodation Support - Partial or full accommodation support (based on availability)
  • Travel Grant - Partial travel grant to help cover transportation costs (based on availability and need)
  • Mentorship Opportunities - Access to mentorship sessions with senior researchers
  • Networking Events - Invitation to exclusive student networking events

Eligibility Criteria#

To be eligible for the scholarship, applicants must:

  1. Be currently enrolled as a graduate or undergraduate student at an accredited institution
  2. Demonstrate financial need
  3. Have a strong interest in AI and ML research
  4. Submit a complete application package by the deadline

Priority will be given to:

  • Students with accepted papers at GPEML
  • Students from underrepresented groups
  • Students from developing countries
  • First-time conference attendees

Application Requirements#

Applicants must submit the following materials:

  1. Application Form - Complete the online application form
  2. Student Status Verification
    • Current transcript or enrollment certificate
    • Letter from academic advisor confirming student status
  3. Letter of Recommendation
    • From academic advisor or faculty member
    • Should address academic performance and research potential
  4. Statement of Purpose (1-2 pages)
    • Why you want to attend GPEML
    • How attending will benefit your research and career
    • Financial need statement
  5. CV/Resume - Current curriculum vitae or resume
  6. Paper Acceptance (if applicable)
    • Copy of paper acceptance notification (if you have an accepted paper)

Application Process#

  1. Complete Application Form

    • Fill out the online application form
    • Upload all required documents

  2. Submit Application

    • Submit by the deadline
    • Ensure all materials are complete

  3. Review Process

    • Applications will be reviewed by the scholarship committee
    • Decisions will be based on merit, need, and diversity

  4. Notification

    • Applicants will be notified of decisions via email
    • Successful applicants will receive detailed information about benefits

Important Dates#

  • Application Deadline: To be announced
  • Notification Date: To be announced
  • Acceptance Deadline: To be announced

Terms and Conditions#

  • Scholarship recipients must attend the full conference
  • Recipients may be asked to volunteer at the conference (2-4 hours)
  • Recipients must acknowledge the scholarship in any publications or presentations
  • Failure to attend the conference may result in repayment of scholarship funds
