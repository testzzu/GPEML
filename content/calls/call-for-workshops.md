## Call for Workshops

We invite proposals for workshops to be held in conjunction with GPEML. Workshops provide a forum for discussion of specialized topics and offer an opportunity for focused interaction among participants.

### Workshop Topics

Workshops can cover any topic related to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, including:

- Emerging research areas
- Interdisciplinary applications
- Industry-focused topics
- Educational initiatives
- Open problems and challenges

### Proposal Requirements

Workshop proposals should include the following information:

1. **Title and Description**
   - Workshop title
   - Brief description (200-300 words)
   - Topics to be covered
   - Relevance to the AI/ML community

2. **Organizers' Information**
   - Names and affiliations of all organizers
   - Brief biography of each organizer
   - Contact information

3. **Expected Participation**
   - Expected number of participants
   - Target audience
   - Call for papers/contributions (if applicable)

4. **Format and Schedule**
   - Proposed format (full day, half day)
   - Tentative schedule
   - Special requirements (if any)

5. **Previous Editions** (if applicable)
   - History of the workshop
   - Previous attendance numbers
   - Links to previous editions

### Submission Guidelines

- Workshop proposals should be submitted in PDF format
- Maximum length: 4 pages
- Submit via email to: workshops@gpeml2025.org

### Important Dates

- **Workshop Proposal Submission Deadline:** April 22, 2025
- **Workshop Proposal Notification:** May 15, 2025
- **Workshop Paper Submission Deadline:** To be determined by workshop organizers
- **Workshop Date:** November 17, 2025 (tentative)

### Workshop Organization

Accepted workshops will be responsible for:

- Organizing their own review process
- Managing paper submissions and reviews
- Preparing workshop proceedings (if applicable)
- Coordinating with conference organizers

### Support

The conference will provide:

- Meeting room and basic AV equipment
- Coffee breaks
- Publication of workshop proceedings (if applicable)
- Publicity through conference channels

### Contact

For workshop-related inquiries, please contact: workshops@gpeml2025.org
