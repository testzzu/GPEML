## Call for Papers

The GPEML Program Committee invites original, substantial, and unpublished technical papers on all areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

### Topics of Interest

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

- **Machine Learning**
  - Supervised learning
  - Unsupervised learning
  - Reinforcement learning
  - Transfer learning
  - Meta-learning

- **Deep Learning**
  - Neural network architectures
  - Convolutional neural networks
  - Recurrent neural networks
  - Generative models
  - Deep reinforcement learning

- **Natural Language Processing**
  - Language models
  - Machine translation
  - Text classification
  - Question answering
  - Dialogue systems

- **Computer Vision**
  - Image classification
  - Object detection
  - Semantic segmentation
  - Video analysis
  - 3D vision

- **Robotics**
  - Robot learning
  - Motion planning
  - Human-robot interaction
  - Autonomous systems

- **Knowledge Representation and Reasoning**
  - Ontologies
  - Semantic web
  - Automated reasoning
  - Knowledge graphs

- **Planning and Scheduling**
  - Automated planning
  - Task scheduling
  - Resource allocation

- **Multi-agent Systems**
  - Agent coordination
  - Game theory
  - Distributed AI

- **AI Applications**
  - Healthcare AI
  - Education
  - Finance
  - Transportation
  - Smart cities

### Submission Guidelines

#### Format Requirements

- **Format:** Springer LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science) format
- **Page Limit:** 
  - Regular papers: Maximum 12 pages (including references)
  - Short papers: Maximum 6 pages (including references)
- **File Format:** PDF only
- **Language:** English

#### Paper Categories

1. **Regular Papers** - Full research papers presenting substantial contributions
2. **Short Papers** - Work-in-progress, position papers, or preliminary results

#### Review Process

- All papers will undergo a **double-blind review** process
- Each paper will be reviewed by at least three program committee members
- Reviews will be based on:
  - Originality and novelty
  - Technical quality and rigor
  - Significance and impact
  - Clarity and presentation

#### Submission Requirements

- Papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference
- Previously published work or work under review elsewhere is not acceptable
- All author information must be removed for blind review
- Papers must be submitted in PDF format through the conference submission system

### Important Dates

- **Paper Submission Deadline:** June 13, 2025
- **Extended Paper Submission Deadline:** June 27, 2025 (Anywhere on Earth)
- **Acceptance Notification:** August 22, 2025
- **Camera-Ready Submission:** September 5, 2025
