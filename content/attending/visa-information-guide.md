International attendees may require a visa to enter the host country. Please review the visa requirements for your passport well in advance and start the application process as soon as registration is confirmed.

### Visa Support Letters

The organising committee can provide visa support letters for registered attendees. To request a letter:

1. Complete your workshop registration  
2. Email `visa@gpeml2025.org`  
3. Include your registration confirmation and passport information
